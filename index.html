About Us

V Room Studios based in Coimbatore is an independent full-service video production company. We make content with impact from promos to documentaries, animations, event films and more. Trusted by clients across various fields like the arts, corporate, medical, education, and public sectors since 2018. We will deliver the videos that reach your goals.

Our Vision and Mission

V Room Studios aims to be a leading moving image production company for directly commissioned content. Through V Room Studios workshops, we want to reach out to thousands of young people and adults each year, Empowering them to be creative through fun and accessible filmmaking experiences.

We Focus On

  • Higher Quality production output.
  • On Time Delivery.
  • Client Satisfaction.
  • Value for Money.
  • Giving access to the source file.

Ambition and Responsibility

We are proudly ambitious, for our clients, the company and ourselves. We are responsible filmmakers, editors and facilitators who feel ownership for anything we do.

Services

Our Production Service Include

Corporate Videos

At V Room Studios, we offer a vast range of corporate video services, from conference filming to animated explainers. With the Business to Business and Business to Customer video, achieve what you want – and more.

Animated Explainer Videos

Our experienced team of writers, motion graphics designers and animators are experts at designing and telling your story. Using attractive design and careful scripting, we will create video content that is ideal for your website, social platforms or marketing campaigns.

Educational Videos

At V Room Studios, we will make your training content informative, educational and effective. We have produced learning and information videos for the healthcare, public, arts, education sectors and more.

Promotional Videos

Undoubtedly, promotional videos have become very popular, especially for businesses. Also, this is because powerful mobile devices and fast wireless internet are now easily accessible. Most businesses have come to realize that using videos for marketing is very affordable with a great return on their investment. 

Documentary

A powerful, three-minute film can move and inspire. It could open the audience’s eyes to a human interest story, communicate the mission of your charity campaign, or uncover the history of an artwork.

Training

We offer skills-focused workshops to organisations who are looking to produce video content in-house by training or upscaling their workforce. We have been training people with little or no experience to make great videos since 2018 and our approach ranges from one-day workshops to Continuing Professional Development programmes.

Video Editing

When it comes to video editing V Room Studios we have the necessary skills & resources to transform & edit different kinds of raw footage into perhaps professional videos within a very short time. So, if you have large footage in any format such as MOV, AVI, WMV etc.

Social Media Videos

Video is without any doubt one of the fastest-growing media. Mix it up with social media and it can spice up your social media content showering you with competitive advantage & perhaps a marketing relevance.
